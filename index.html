Who we are ?

We are a local company, which provides domiciliary service to medium and small stores.

Why we decided to create this company

Due to the increasing digitization, it is becoming almost indispensable for any company, no matter how small, to join this trend. Small companies, however, find it very difficult to do so, that is why we created domiexpress a company that aims to provide advertising through our website, as well as the logistics service for home deliveries.

Our mision

We want to bring the best of the digital world to our community and with this encourage commerce. Our goal is that small businesses in the area can have access to these technologies without the need to invest large sums in infrastructure and specialized personnel.

bussiness model

Our business model focuses on two fronts, on the one hand we design a platform where stores can display their products and prices, in addition our platform has a shopping cart with which the customer can select the product and place an order. to the respective store
